# OVERALL POSITION

VaultEdge Financial Technologies adopts a **moderate risk appetite** in support of innovation, rapid growth, and international market expansion. As a fintech organization operating across multiple jurisdictions and processing sensitive customer and payment data, VaultEdge recognizes that certain operational and technology risks are unavoidable in achieving strategic objectives.

However, VaultEdge maintains **low tolerance for risks that could result in regulatory violations, customer data compromise, significant financial loss, operational disruption, or reputational damage.** Risks impacting customer trust, legal obligations, payment security, and business continuity must be actively identified, managed, monitored, and reduced to acceptable levels through governance, security controls, and continuous oversight.

---

# RISK CATEGORY APPETITES

| RISK CATEGORY               | APPETITE LEVEL | STATEMENT                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            |
| --------------------------- | -------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Customer Data Security**  | **Low**        | VaultEdge has minimal tolerance for risks that may compromise customer personal data, payment information, authentication credentials, or sensitive business information. Security controls, encryption, monitoring, access management, and incident response capabilities must be implemented to reduce the likelihood and impact of data breaches. |
| **Regulatory Compliance**   | **Low**        | VaultEdge maintains minimal tolerance for non-compliance with GDPR, PCI-DSS, UK FCA regulations, Kenya Data Protection Act, Singapore PDPA, and contractual security obligations. Regulatory requirements must be proactively monitored and addressed to avoid fines, sanctions, legal exposure, and loss of customer trust.                         |
| **Technology & Innovation** | **Moderate**   | VaultEdge accepts calculated and managed technology risks to support innovation, product development, cloud adoption, market expansion, and competitive advantage. Emerging technologies may be adopted provided that appropriate risk assessments, testing, governance, and security reviews are conducted.                                         |
| **Operational Continuity**  | **Low**        | VaultEdge has limited tolerance for operational disruptions affecting payment processing, customer access, API availability, or critical business operations. Business continuity, disaster recovery, backup, and incident management capabilities must be established to ensure resilience and service availability.                                |
| **Financial**               | **Moderate**   | VaultEdge accepts measured financial risk associated with strategic investments, security improvements, expansion initiatives, and technology modernization. However, uncontrolled financial losses resulting from weak governance, fraud, compliance failures, or major incidents are unacceptable.                                                 |
| **Reputational**            | **Low**        | VaultEdge has minimal tolerance for risks that could significantly damage customer trust, investor confidence, regulatory relationships, or market reputation. Security incidents, regulatory breaches, unethical conduct, or poor governance practices that negatively impact public confidence must be prevented and addressed promptly.           |

# Additional Governance Notes

## Zero-Tolerance Areas

VaultEdge maintains zero tolerance for:

- intentional regulatory violations,
- fraudulent activity,
- unauthorized disclosure of payment card data,
- deliberate bypass of security controls,
- and unethical handling of customer information.

---

## Risk Ownership

All business units are responsible for identifying and managing risks within their operations, while the GRC function, CISO, and executive leadership provide governance oversight, monitoring, and reporting to the Board of Directors.

---

# Why This Risk Appetite Statement Fits VaultEdge

This structure aligns with VaultEdge’s:

- fintech operating model,
- international regulatory exposure,
- investor expectations,
- ISO 27001 certification goal,
- and recent governance failures.

It also reflects the reality that:

- fintech companies must innovate quickly,
- but cannot afford weak governance around customer data, compliance, and operational resilience.
